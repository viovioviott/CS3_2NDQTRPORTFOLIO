How to survive Pisay? Well, here are some recommended hangout spots from yours truly!!!

FLOB and BLOB

aka the Front and Back Lobbies

bench seatings around the segments between the admin and SHB buildings and by the back of the SHB

Pros:

  • Spacious and open air
  • Free seatings and charging ports
  • Easy to access and free to all
  • A gadget friendly place

Cons:

  • Can be crowded
  • Areas may be messy and loud
  • Stray cats tend to roam around these areas

Notes:

  • the BLOB also functions as a canteen
  • offers food but is mostly loud and crowded

Gazebos

scattered seating around the drop off and back field areas

Pros:

  • Plenty of space and specially built for groups of people
  • Open aired and plenty in different areas
  • Easy to access and free to all
  • A gadget friendly place

Cons:

  • Potentially dirty or seats can be chipped
  • Stray cats tend to roam around these areas

Grandstand

an open, seated area fit for any pisay student

Pros:

  • Open aired and full of seats and tables
  • Offers a lovely view of the campus
  • A covered area fit for some sports under the sun
  • A gadget friendly place

Cons:

  • Can be crowded at times
  • Quite dirty, as seats also can be subbed as steps for each level

Library

a cold, cushoined area fit for studying and working in peace

Pros:

  • Air conditioned and cushioned
  • Filled with information, books, computers and charging ports
  • Allows for individual study areas
  • Has rooms for groups of 4 to 6 people
  • A mostly quiet place
  • A gadget friendly place

Cons:

  • Can be crowded at times
  • Silence must be observed

Josies Nook

a brand new air conditioned room specially built for students to stay in

Pros:

  • Specially built for students, has art materials, games, etc.
  • Soft seatings (sofas, beanbags) and round tables and benches
  • Air conditioned and relaxing

Cons:

  • Can be crowded at times
  • Not always open/ open on specific times only
  • Gadgets are not allowed
